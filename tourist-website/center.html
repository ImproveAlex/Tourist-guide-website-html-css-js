Central area

The central area isn't generally high on international tourists minds, except for Chicago, as a place of interest. Generally thought of as being flat and dull, with row after row of corn as "high as an elephant's eye." But the central ar includes some amazing sights that are off the usual tourist track, and some of the great outdoor adventures for the camper, hiker or kayaker.
